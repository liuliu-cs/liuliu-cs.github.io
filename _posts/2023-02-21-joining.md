**On joining the [EPIC-Lab](https://epic-rpi.github.io)**

We are looking for highly self-motivated Ph.D. students and postdocs to work on **Elastic AI Computing** via *algorithm-architecture co-design*. Prospective students are encouraged to apply to Ph.D. in Computer and Systems Engineering or in Computer Science at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. RPI was ranked 41 in Computer Engineering and 60 in Computer Science by U.S. News 2024.

**To prospective students and visitors:**

Send a short introductory email to: epic.ecse@gmail.com. Prepare the following:
* Read one recent paper from [publications](/publications)
* Explain specifically what you read
* Relate to your background and interests
* Concisely describe your insight
* Attach your CV, transcripts, and representative publication (if any)


**Looking for students interested in the following research topics:**

*At the intersection of computer systems and AI/ML:*
* Specialized hardware architectures
* Hardware-efficient ML methods
* Efficient model training, inference, and serving
* Machine Learning for Systems

*Privacy & Security in computing*

*Architecture for Robotics & Autonomous Systems*
            
**Required Background and Qualifications**
* Solid programming and math skills.
* Knowledge in computer architecture and systems; familiar with digital design and synthesis (HDL); Experience with architectural simulation or design prototyping on FPGAs is a plus.
* Knowledge in machine learning; familiar with ML frameworks (PyTorch/TensorFlow/JAX).
